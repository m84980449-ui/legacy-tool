How Binance Fees Work

  • Maker: You place a limit order that sits in the order book. Lower fee because you add liquidity.
  • Taker: You execute a market order that fills immediately. Slightly higher fee.
  • BNB Discount: Enable "Use BNB for fees" in your account settings to get 25% off automatically.
  • VIP Levels: Determined by your 30-day trading volume in BTC equivalent. Updates daily.
