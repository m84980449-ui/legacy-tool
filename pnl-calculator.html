Understanding Your P&L

  • Position Value: Your margin × leverage. This is the actual size of your trade in the market.
  • Gross P&L: Raw profit/loss before fees — (Exit − Entry) × Position Size for longs.
  • Net P&L: Gross P&L minus fees paid on entry and exit.
  • ROI: Net P&L ÷ Margin × 100. This is your actual return on the capital you put in.
  • Liquidation Price: Estimated price where your margin is fully consumed. Always leave buffer.
  • Break-Even: The price you need to exit at to cover fees exactly (neither profit nor loss).

Key insight: With 10× leverage, a 10% move in your favor gives you ~100% ROI. But a 10% move against you = liquidation. Always set a stop-loss before entering a leveraged trade.
